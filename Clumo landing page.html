Why Clumo

Never miss an opportunity

Ever felt that moment of panic in a sales call where you don't know the answer? Or perhaps you missed the opportunity to ask the killer question? Or talk about the perfect case study? That's where Clumo helps. It's like having an A-player on every call with you.

Realtime coaching
Suggestions streamed as you talk — discovery questions, case studies, and proof points surfaced at exactly the right moment. Not after the call. During it.
Your private knowledge base
Train Clumo with your website, battle cards, and docs. Your KB is stored locally on your machine — private, encrypted, and never shared. Only used by your instance of Clumo.
Inbuilt sales frameworks
Choose from MEDDPICC, SPIN, or BANT. Clumo tracks qualification criteria in real-time as the conversation progresses. See gaps live, not in a post-call review.
Bring your own keys
Works with Azure OpenAI or OpenAI. Your keys, your data, your costs. Keys are encrypted at rest on your machine. No middleman, no data sharing, no telemetry.
Windows · macOS · Linux
Desktop app that works with any meeting platform — Zoom, Teams, Meet, or anything running on your machine. No browser extension. No Docker. Just download and run.
Post-call analysis
Automatic call notes, CRM-ready summaries, and follow-up emails generated from the full conversation context.

Get started

Up and running in 5 minutes

Download the app, paste your API key, and train it with your website. That's it.

quickstart
# Get up and running in 5 minutes.

1. Download Clumo for your platform
   Grab the installer from GitHub Releases — Windows, macOS, or Linux.

2. Paste your API key
   Azure OpenAI or OpenAI. Your keys stay encrypted on your machine.

3. Train with your website & documents
   Enter a URL or upload battle cards, case studies, playbooks. Clumo builds your KB.

4. Join a call & start coaching
   Pick the audio source, hit Start, and let Clumo do the rest.
